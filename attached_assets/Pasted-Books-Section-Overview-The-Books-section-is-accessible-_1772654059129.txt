Books Section Overview

The Books section is accessible via a "Books" navigation item and contains four modules:
1. Asset Register (/assets)

Purpose: Track all fixed assets owned by the business.

Asset Categories:

    Building (10% depreciation)
    Plant & Machinery (15% depreciation)
    Furniture & Fixtures (10% depreciation)
    Vehicles (15% depreciation)
    Computers (40% depreciation)
    Electrical Fittings (10% depreciation)
    Other (10% depreciation)

Features:

    Full CRUD (create, read, update, delete) for assets
    Two types of assets: "Opening" (pre-existing assets entered manually) and assets created automatically via capital expenses
    Each asset tracks: name, category, purchase date, original cost, current book value, depreciation rate, and disposal info
    Disposal tracking: disposal date, disposal amount, and reason
    Assets created from capital expenses are linked back to the expense record via expenseId

Depreciation Engine:

    Written Down Value (WDV) method per Indian Income Tax Act rates
    Financial Year (April 1 – March 31) based calculation
    Month-based proration: e.g., asset purchased in November gets 5 months of depreciation in that FY
    Idempotent: if run again for the same FY, it recalculates rather than doubling
    Depreciation log stored in assetDepreciationLog table with: asset ID, FY, opening value, depreciation amount, closing value, months used

2. Liability Register (/liabilities)

Purpose: Track all liabilities/loans owed by the business.

Liability Types:

    Bank Loan
    Equipment Loan
    Credit Line
    Outstanding Payable
    Other

Features:

    Full CRUD for liabilities
    Each liability tracks: name, type, original amount, outstanding amount, interest rate, EMI amount, start date, settlement status
    Payment recording with principal/interest split
    Payment reversals
    Settlement tracking (mark as settled)

3. Balance Sheet (/balance-sheet)

Purpose: FY-based financial position report as of March 31.

Structure:

Fixed Assets (by category):

    Shows each asset category's total after depreciation for the selected FY
    Uses the depreciation engine to compute book values

Current Assets:

    Farmer PY Receivables — outstanding opening receivable balances from farmers
    Buyer PY Receivables — outstanding opening receivable balances from buyers/merchants
    Farmer Advance — outstanding advance amounts given to farmers (from farmer_advance_freight table, type = 'advance')
    Farmer Freight — outstanding freight amounts for farmers (from farmer_advance_freight table, type = 'freight')
    Merchant Advance — outstanding advance amounts given to merchants/buyers (from merchant_advance table)
    Cash in Hand — from opening balance settings
    Bank Account Balances — computed from transactions (opening balance + receipts - expenses + transfers in - transfers out)

Long-term Liabilities:

    From the liability register (bank loans, equipment loans, etc.)

Current Liabilities:

    Includes auto-calculated limit account outstanding: if a bank account of type "limit" has a negative balance (computed as openingBalance + receipts - expenses + transfersIn - transfersOut), the absolute value is shown as a current liability

Owner's Equity:

    Equity = Total Assets (Fixed + Current) − Total Liabilities (Long-term + Current)

Export: CSV download via ?format=csv
4. Profit & Loss (/profit-and-loss)

Purpose: FY-based income and expense report.

Income:

    Cold Storage Charges = sum of coldStorageCharge from sales − sum of adjReceivableSelfDueAmount (adjustments for self-sale dues)
    Receivable Adjustments (PY receivable adjustments, advance adjustments, freight adjustments) — shown as deductions
    Merchant Extras (extraDueToMerchant) — additional charges billed to merchants
    Other Income — any income categorized as "other_income"
    Total Income = Cold Storage Charges − Receivable Adjustments + Merchant Extras + Other Income

Expenses:

    Revenue expenses only (expenseClass = 'revenue'), grouped by type:
        Salary, Hammali, Grading Charges, General Expenses, Interest on Loan, Electricity Charges, Chemical Spray Charges
    Depreciation — total depreciation for the FY from the depreciation engine
    Interest on Liabilities — interest portion of liability payments during the FY
    Capital expenses (expenseClass = 'capital') and Advance expenses (expenseClass = 'advance') are excluded from P&L

Net Profit/Loss: Total Income − Total Expenses

Export: CSV download via ?format=csv
Year Convention

    All four modules use Indian Financial Year (April 1 – March 31)
    FY labels follow "YYYY-YY" format (e.g., "2025-26")
    Balance Sheet date is always March 31 of the FY end year
    Depreciation proration is based on months within the FY

Data Tables Involved

    assets — fixed asset records
    assetDepreciationLog — yearly depreciation entries per asset
    liabilities — liability/loan records
    liabilityPayments — payment records against liabilities (with principal/interest split)
    openingReceivables — PY receivable balances (farmer and buyer)
    farmerAdvanceFreight — farmer advance and freight records
    merchantAdvance — merchant/buyer advance records
    expenses — all expense records (with expenseClass distinguishing revenue/capital/advance)
    cashReceipts — income records
    salesHistory — sales records (source of cold storage charges)
    bankAccounts — bank account definitions and opening balances
    cashTransfers — inter-account transfers
